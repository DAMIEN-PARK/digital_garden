### 최근 수정 도서

```dataview

TABLE WITHOUT ID

    "![|60](" + cover + ")" as Cover,

    link(file.link, title) as Title,

    join(list(author)) as Author,

    status as Status

FROM #Book

WHERE !contains(file.path, "Templates")

SORT status DESC, file.mtime DESC

LIMIT 10

```
